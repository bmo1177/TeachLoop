# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mixed audience of professionals improving their communication skills: software engineers practicing technical communication, students learning to explain complex concepts, job seekers preparing for technical interviews, and anyone wanting to articulate ideas more clearly to different audiences.

## Product Purpose

TeachLoop helps people practice explaining technical concepts (AI/ML, system design) to different audiences, providing AI-powered feedback on communication style. The goal is to build confidence and clarity in how users articulate ideas, not just what they know.

## Brand Personality

Warm encouraging coach. Friendly, supportive, growth-focused. Like a patient mentor who believes in your potential while gently pointing out areas to improve.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS templates. Avoid the "AI tool" aesthetic: neon accents on dark backgrounds, chatbot-centric layouts, feature grids with identical icon cards, or any interface that feels mass-produced rather than thoughtfully designed.

## Design Principles

1. **Coach over critic**: Feedback should feel encouraging, not judgmental. Every interaction reinforces growth.
2. **Focus on the human**: The interface disappears; the user's words and communication are the focus.
3. **Progress visible**: Always show where the user is in their journey (progress bars, scores, streaks).
4. **Warmth through craft**: Personality comes from thoughtful details (microcopy, animations, transitions), not visual noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AAA compliance. Support reduced motion preferences. Ensure screen reader compatibility, keyboard navigation, sufficient color contrast, and focus indicators throughout.
